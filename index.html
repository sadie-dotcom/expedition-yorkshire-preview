The people who make the day

Meet your driver-guides

Not a rota of strangers — a small team who live here and love showing it off. You'll know who's driving before you arrive.

Andrew

Founder & Lead Driver-Guide

Born and raised on the edge of the Moors, Andrew started Expedition Yorkshire to show visitors the county the way a friend would. Twenty years behind the wheel, a bottomless store of local stories, and a knack for finding the quiet viewpoint just as the light turns.

Richard

Senior Driver-Guide

A historian by training and a Dales walker by habit, Richard is the one to ask about the abbeys, the castles and which village pub does the best lunch. Calm, unhurried and endlessly patient — families and first-time visitors always ask for him again.
