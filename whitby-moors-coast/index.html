Highlights

Whitby Abbey & harbour · the North York Moors · the steam railway at Goathland · Helmsley.

Ideal for

Couples and families who want the coast, the moors and a little history in one unhurried day.

The Whitby, Moors & Coast tour is a private, full-day driver-guided trip from York for up to 7 guests. You travel across the North York Moors to the coast at Whitby — with its abbey and harbour — and the steam railway at Goathland, in a Mercedes-Benz V-Class with hotel pickup. One price covers the whole group, from £985 including VAT.

The day, stop by stop

A typical route — yours to adjust

A guideline, not a timetable. We shape the order and pace around your group on the day.

Stop 1

Helmsley

A handsome market town to begin — coffee in the square, the castle ruins, and the road out onto the moors.

Stop 2

North York Moors

Open moor-top driving across the high heather, with the big skies and viewpoints that make this landscape.

Stop 3

Whitby

Down to the coast: the harbour, the 199 steps and the abbey on the headland, with time to explore and eat.

Stop 4

Goathland & the steam railway

The steam railway and the village made famous on screen, before an unhurried run back to York.
