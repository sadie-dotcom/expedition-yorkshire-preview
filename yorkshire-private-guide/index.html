Expedition Yorkshire's private guides are local driver-guides — including founder Andrew Ward and Richard — who lead private, full-day tours for up to 7 guests. They drive, guide and tailor the day to your group, so you get a real local's knowledge rather than a script.

The guides are the product

Meet the people who'll show you Yorkshire

Our guides don't read from a script. They know the history, the landscapes and the corners most visitors miss — and they adjust the day as they read your group.

Founder

Andrew started Expedition Yorkshire to share the county the way a local sees it. You may have heard him on the podcast that first brought many guests to us.

Driver-guide
